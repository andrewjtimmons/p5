After the universe is gone

An exploration of the dimensionality of color

Pixels have 3 color channels, red, green, and blue, each with 256 possible values.
Therefore pixels have 256 to the power of 3 (which is 16,777,216) possible colors.
This 4x4 pixel image starts with random colors and has 256 to the power of 48 unique combinations.
That is about equal the number 4 followed by one hundred and fifteen zeros.
For comparison ten billion is only 1 followed by ten zeros.
It loops through all 16,777,216 colors for the first pixel before it changes any colors in the second pixel.
And it loops through all colors in the second pixel before changing the third, and so on and so forth.
This image will update around 60 times a second.
It will take about 1 followed by one hundred and eight zeros years to complete.
Scientists estimate the universe will end in about 2 followed by ten zeros years.
